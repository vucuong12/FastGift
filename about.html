You have a girlfriend’s birthday?

Easily make an interactive e-giftcard containing her photos!

You can choose the template with pre-made storylines. Then you make a giftcard based on that. You don’t have to be a poet. You can simply type some messages and input photos/videos/sounds on the pre-made templates. After finish making, you can manage all the giftcards you made easily in ‘MyGift’. Finally send the precious giftcard’s URL to your lovely GF!
