You have a girlfriend’s birthday?

Easily make an interactive e-giftcard containing her photos!

1. You can choose the template with pre-made storylines. Then you make a giftcard based on that. You don’t have to be a poet. You can simply type some messages and input photos/videos on the pre-made templates.

2. After finish making, you can manage all the giftcards you made easily in ‘MyGifts’.

3. Finally send the precious giftcard’s URL to your lovely GF!
